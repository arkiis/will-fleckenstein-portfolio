Define the problem
Roommate tension often begins with miscommunications and
unclear expectations about shared responsibilities.

When roommates avoid talking about these issues, frustration builds,
leading to feelings of unfairness and mistrust over time.

PAGE 11
----------------------------------------
Challenges:(
How we Solved them
Identifying the Real Problem

Determining why roommate conflicts happen, not just what the conflicts were about.
Conducted a short survey focusing on three common areas: room temperature,
cleaning responsibilities, and shared supply costs.

Understanding Conflict Patterns

Tasks being ignored, forgotten, or completed incorrectly.
Many issues escalated because concerns were not communicated clearly or were
repeatedly overlooked.

Balancing Accountability without Tension

Designing a system that promotes accountability without creating resentment between
roommates.
We solved this by adding neutral reminders and shared task visibility, so the app
supports organization rather than blame.

Communication Solutions

Conflicts can be:
Resolved individually (someone fixes the issue themselves).
Solved through communication that leads to a mutual agreement.

Key Design Insight

The biggest problem was tasks being repeatedly ignored, so the app needed
a reminder system to help roommates stay accountable.

Team Collaboration

Our group helped each other refine ideas, share research insights, and test
the prototype.
We also used surveys, UX design resources, and peer feedback to guide our
decisions.
